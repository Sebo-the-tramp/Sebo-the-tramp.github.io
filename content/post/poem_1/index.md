# The time

What is the first result\
on the IG search bar

With whom, you\
saw the sun\
set and raise\
and all again

The smell that is left\
The moment you wait\
For that small circus\
In the stomach to cruise

The person\
that can change\
Your mood\
with just one move

And the time I waited\
upon, that's all I needed
