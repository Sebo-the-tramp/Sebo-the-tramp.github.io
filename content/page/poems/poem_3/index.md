# Some

Some sadness in my hearth\
But I can't feel

My head's on pills\
To gain inner peace

I feel like Leopardi\
There is a bush in front of me\
I can't see clear

I lay on the grass\
Feeling insecure\
You gave me all

I gave you all away

Will I ever be forgiven?
