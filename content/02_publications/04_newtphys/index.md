### Preprint in preparation

**NewtPhys: Do Foundation Models Understand Newtonian Physics?**

*Sebastian Cavada, Soumava Paul, Tuan-Hung Vu, Andrei Bursuc, Raoul de Charette*

## Abstract

NewtPhys is a physics-grounded benchmark for evaluating vision-language and foundation models on low-level Newtonian reasoning. The benchmark contains 11k image sequences and 141k VQA pairs generated from 3D Gaussian Splatting and mesh-free simulation at scale. Initial evaluation of more than 60 models reveals systematic failures in physical reasoning, especially when models must infer dynamics rather than recognize static visual patterns.
